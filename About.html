ABOUT US

The school is both day and boarding for both girls and boys right from nursery upto primary seven.

Our mission is to inspire the young generation while competing at the highest level of integrity.

We have quality teachers, conducive environment, good feeding and electricity is standby generator.

Our school motto is: FOR SECURE FUTURE.

How to Reach Us

School Bus Service

We operate 8 bus routes covering all major neighborhoods. Buses arrive at school by 7:45AM and depart at 3:15PM.

  • Route A: Covers Northside neighborhoods
  • Route B: Serves the downtown area
  • Route C: For students in the eastern suburbs
View Bus Schedule →

Public Transportation

The following options stop within a 5-minute walk from our campus:

  • City Bus: Routes 12, 15, and 22
  • Subway: Green Line - Education Station
  • Commuter Rail: Springfield Central Station

Driving Directions

From downtown Springfield:

  1. Take Main Street east for 1.5 miles
  2. Turn right onto Education Avenue
  3. Continue for 0.3 miles - school is on the left

Free parking is available for visitors in the front lot.

Biking/Walking

We encourage active transportation! Our campus features:

  • Covered bike racks near all entrances
  • Safe walking paths from nearby neighborhoods
  • Shower facilities for students who bike long distances
